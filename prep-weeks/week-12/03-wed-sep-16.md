# Week 12 · Day 3 — Wed Sep 16 — Accept + Offer-Letter Review

📌 **Today:** Read the full offer letter line by line, confirm a start date that leverages your immediate availability, then accept formally and get a countersigned copy. Nothing is real until it's in writing.

**Primary activity:**

**Read the full offer letter (60 min):**
- [ ] Check the **fixed/variable split** — what's guaranteed vs. performance-linked.
- [ ] Check **joining-bonus terms** — is there a clawback period? How long must you stay to keep it?
- [ ] Check **ESOP vesting** — cliff, schedule, strike price, what happens if you leave.
- [ ] Confirm **designation/band**, reporting line, and any conditions or contingencies (BGV clearance, etc.).
- [ ] Note anything ambiguous and ask before signing — not after.

**Confirm start date + accept (rest of day):**
- [ ] Confirm the **start date in writing**. As an immediate joiner you can set it early to win goodwill — but leave yourself a short reset gap if you want one; you've earned a brief breather before day one.
- [ ] Accept formally over **email**, restating the agreed comp components and start date so there's a written record.
- [ ] Get a **countersigned copy** of the offer letter back from them.

**Pipeline:** Once you have a signed offer, tell still-active loops you're close to a decision — this is also a final nudge for any company that could still beat it. Don't decline anyone yet (that's Friday). Log the acceptance in the tracker.

**EOD check:**
- [ ] Did you read every clause — fixed/variable, clawback, ESOP vesting, designation, conditions?
- [ ] Is the start date confirmed in writing, set to leverage immediate availability?
- [ ] Have you accepted by email and requested a countersigned copy?
