# Week 12 · Day 2 — Tue Sep 15 — Negotiate the Lead Offer to Its Ceiling

📌 **Today:** One clean, final negotiation pass on your lead offer. Counter with a specific number, base-first, one reason — and use competing offers plus your immediate availability as leverage. Then hold the silence and let them respond.

**Primary activity:**

**Final negotiation pass on the lead offer (rest of day):**
- [ ] Make one clean counter: a specific number (not a range), one reason, **base salary first**. Reference any competing offer factually, not as a threat.
- [ ] If base is capped by band, pivot the ask to a **joining bonus and/or ESOP** sweetener rather than walking away empty.
- [ ] Offer your immediate start as a concession in exchange for movement on comp — it has real value to a team under headcount pressure, and you've already resigned so there's no notice period to wait out.
- [ ] After you counter, **hold the silence** — don't negotiate against yourself or soften the number before they reply.
- [ ] Evaluate any revised offer on in-hand + guaranteed cash, the same way you ranked the matrix yesterday — not on the headline number.

**Negotiation guardrails:**
- [ ] Keep it to a single counter; multiple rounds of small asks erode goodwill right before you join.
- [ ] Get any agreed change confirmed in writing before you treat it as settled.

**Pipeline:** Keep the other live loops warm while you negotiate — a second real offer is what gives this counter its weight. Reply to recruiters same-day. Log the counter and any response in the tracker.

**EOD check:**
- [ ] Did you make exactly one clean counter — specific number, base-first, one reason?
- [ ] Did you offer immediate start as a concession for movement on comp?
- [ ] Did you hold the silence instead of negotiating against yourself?
