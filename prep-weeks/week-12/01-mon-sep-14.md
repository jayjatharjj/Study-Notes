# Week 12 · Day 1 — Mon Sep 14 — Decision Matrix + Final Leverage Calls

📌 **Today:** The closing week begins. Lay every live offer and loop side by side in one matrix, rank on cash-in-hand first, and run a decision-forcing nudge on every company still mid-loop. Because you've already resigned and can join immediately, use that to compress fence-sitters to a decision this week.

**Primary activity:**

**Build the offer decision matrix (45 min):**
- [ ] Lay out every live offer/loop in one table: company, tier, **monthly in-hand**, **guaranteed annual cash**, variable/ESOP, designation, growth/scope, team signal, decision deadline.
- [ ] Rank on in-hand + guaranteed cash first (not headline CTC), then growth/scope. The biggest CTC is rarely the most in-hand.
- [ ] Confirm any *verbal* offer in writing before you treat it as real on the matrix.
- [ ] Flag the two columns that actually decide it for you (e.g. in-hand and stack growth) so emotion doesn't drift the ranking later in the week.

**Final leverage / nudge calls (rest of day):**
- [ ] For every company still mid-loop, send a decision-forcing nudge — *"I'm close to a decision and [Company] is a top choice; is there any way to expedite the remaining step this week? I've already resigned and can start immediately."*
- [ ] Make immediate availability the headline of every nudge — it's your single strongest lever as an immediate joiner.
- [ ] Set a personal internal deadline (e.g. accept by Sep 20) and let it drive the week's pace.

**Pipeline:** Respond to every recruiter within hours today — speed signals seriousness in the closing window. Log each nudge sent and each reply in the tracker so you can see which loops are actually moving.

**EOD check:**
- [ ] Is every live offer and loop in one ranked matrix, ordered by in-hand + guaranteed cash?
- [ ] Did every mid-loop company get a decision-forcing nudge that surfaces immediate availability?
- [ ] Is every verbal offer now confirmed in writing — or flagged as unconfirmed?
