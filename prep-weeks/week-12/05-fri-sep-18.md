# Week 12 · Day 5 — Fri Sep 18 — Decline Gracefully + Close Loops

📌 **Today:** With the offer accepted, close out everything else cleanly. Decline other offers warmly and specifically, thank every person who helped you by name, and mark final outcomes on every row of the tracker. The network you keep warm today is your next switch's head start.

**Primary activity:**

**Decline other offers professionally:**
- [ ] Send a short, warm, **specific** decline to every remaining offer — name something you genuinely valued about the process. You may want these companies later; leave the door open.
- [ ] Decline by the channel they used (email to recruiter/HM), promptly — don't ghost a company that invested rounds in you.
- [ ] Keep it gracious and final; don't over-explain or relitigate comp.

**Thank everyone who helped:**
- [ ] Thank every **referrer** and helper **by name** — a real message, not a blast. This is the relationship capital that gets your next role faster.
- [ ] Tell key contacts where you landed; a closed loop with good news is what makes them refer you again.

**Close the tracker:**
- [ ] Update the tracker: mark final outcomes (accepted / declined / closed) on **every** row.
- [ ] Note any company worth re-approaching in future and why — your future self will thank you.

**Pipeline:** This is the wind-down of the pipeline, not new outreach. The only exception is if you have *no* offer yet — then skip the declines and roll into tomorrow's fallback sprint instead.

**EOD check:**
- [ ] Did every other offer get a warm, specific decline that keeps the door open?
- [ ] Did you thank every referrer and helper by name?
- [ ] Is every tracker row marked with a final outcome?
