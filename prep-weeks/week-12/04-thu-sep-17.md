# Week 12 · Day 4 — Thu Sep 17 — BGV & Document Prep

📌 **Today:** Assemble the full background-verification document set in one place so onboarding doesn't stall. Since you've already resigned, your relieving and experience letters are the most common BGV blocker — chase them down now.

**Primary activity:**

**BGV document checklist — one folder, digital + physical:**
- [ ] **Employment:** prior offer letter(s), **relieving letter**, **experience letter** from previous employer.
- [ ] **Salary proof:** last 3 salary slips, **Form 16 / ITR**, latest bank statement showing salary credits.
- [ ] **Education:** 10th & 12th certificates + marksheets, degree certificate + consolidated marksheet, any post-grad/diploma.
- [ ] **Identity:** PAN card, Aadhaar, passport (if available).
- [ ] **Address proof:** Aadhaar / utility bill / rental agreement matching your current address.
- [ ] **Photos:** a few recent passport-size photos (often needed for onboarding).

**Close the resignation-related gaps (immediate joiner):**
- [ ] You've already resigned — make sure your **relieving + experience letter** from the previous employer is in hand or has a confirmed date. This is the #1 thing that delays BGV; don't let it slip.
- [ ] If the relieving letter is delayed, get a written acknowledgement of resignation/last working day as an interim proof.
- [ ] Confirm the new company's **BGV vendor and timeline** so you know what they'll verify and by when.

**Pipeline:** No new applications today unless a fallback is needed. Reply to any recruiter same-day. Note in the tracker which documents are ready and which are still pending.

**EOD check:**
- [ ] Is every BGV document in one folder (digital + physical), nothing missing?
- [ ] Are the relieving + experience letters in hand or dated — the most common blocker resolved?
- [ ] Do you know the new company's BGV vendor and timeline?
