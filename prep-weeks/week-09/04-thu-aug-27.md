# Week 9 · Day 4 — Thu Aug 27 — Live Round Execution Day

📌 **Today:** This is the template for any actual interview day. Re-read the company brief and JD top-3, know 3 project metrics cold, warm up with one Easy LC — then execute. Thank-you note and written debrief within 12 hours, non-negotiable.

**DSA maintenance (~30 min):**
- [ ] If you had a live round, do this **after** the debrief — not before.
- [ ] Pick one problem the interview made you think about. Solve it properly; reinforce the pattern.

**Primary activity:**

**2 hours before the round:**
- [ ] Re-read your one-page company brief (stack, product, recent news, one observation).
- [ ] Re-read the JD — highlight the **top 3 skills** they're testing for.
- [ ] Re-read the deep-dive for the most relevant project (Smart360 or Deep Fathom). Know **3 metrics cold**.
- [ ] Do NOT cram new material. If you don't know it now, 2 hours won't fix it — warm up instead.

**30 min before:**
- [ ] Solve one Easy LC to warm up pattern recognition — get into "coding mode."
- [ ] Water. Check setup: mic, camera, stable internet, quiet room; all tabs closed except the interview tool and notes (if allowed).

**During the round:**
- [ ] **First 60s:** "tell me about yourself" → 60-second quantified version, ending "I'm excited about [company] specifically because [tailored reason]."
- [ ] **Clarify before coding** — even one question buys thinking time and shows structure.
- [ ] **Narrate your thinking** — silence > 30s is a red flag.
- [ ] **State complexity proactively** — "O(n log n) time, O(n) space — the log n is the sort." Don't wait to be asked.
- [ ] **End with good questions** — 2–3 real ones. Surface immediate availability if start dates come up: "available to join immediately."

**Within 12 hours after:**
- [ ] Thank-you note to recruiter (CC interviewer if you have the email): 3–4 sentences — thank, reference one specific thing from the conversation, reiterate fit, express genuine interest.
- [ ] Debrief (10 min): what you handled well, what you stumbled on, what they cared most about, what to change, what to review before the next round with this company.
- [ ] Update the pipeline row: today's stage, next step, debrief summary.

**Pipeline:** Log the round outcome the same day. If this round opens a next step, schedule it before end of day.

**EOD check:**
- [ ] Thank-you note out within 12 hours — yes/no?
- [ ] Pipeline sheet updated — yes/no?
- [ ] Debrief written — yes/no?
- [ ] DSA reinforcement done (after the debrief)?
