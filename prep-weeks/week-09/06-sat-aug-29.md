# Week 9 · Day 6 — Sat Aug 29 — Per-Company Tailoring + First-Offer Readiness

📌 **Today:** Deep per-company tailoring — bridge answers for every tech gap, a real product observation, tailored questions — plus the first-offer response protocol. First offers can land this week; the moment one does, it becomes leverage to compress every other loop.

**DSA maintenance (~30 min):**
- [ ] **LC 98 (Validate BST)** or **LC 102 (Level Order Traversal)** — frequent in final rounds. Clean solve, narrate aloud.

**Primary activity:**

**Per-company deep tailoring (90 min) — for each company with a round in the next 7 days:**
- [ ] **Tech-stack audit (15 min/company):** map their stack to your experience. For each gap, prep a bridge answer: "I haven't used Kafka directly, but I've implemented the same async decoupling with event-driven architecture — consumer groups and offset management are familiar from studying it. I'd be productive quickly."
- [ ] **Product observation (10 min/company):** read one recent blog post / product update. Prep one genuine observation: "I noticed you migrated to X — curious what drove that over Y. In Deep Fathom we faced a similar choice and..."
- [ ] **Questions you'll ask (10 min/company):** write 5 specific questions; use 2–3 live. Bad: "What does success look like?" Good: "Your post on monolith → microservices — what's the biggest operational surprise post-migration you didn't anticipate?"

**First-offer response protocol (30 min):**
- [ ] When an offer arrives — even a great one — **never accept on the spot.** Use this line: *"Thank you so much — I'm genuinely excited. I'd like to review everything carefully. Can I get back to you by [specific date, 3–5 business days out]?"* Every legitimate company says yes.
- [ ] The first offer is leverage. The moment one lands, every other live loop gets a compression nudge: *"I've received an offer I'm evaluating with a decision window of about a week. [Company] is genuinely a top choice — is there any way to accelerate the remaining steps?"*
- [ ] Quick first-pass eval (full framework is Week 10): fixed base at/above floor? Variable/bonus structure? Joining bonus possible? ESOP/LTI vesting? Growth/band? Location/hybrid? BGV doc requirements?
- [ ] Don't judge on prestige alone — a ₹22 LPA offer at an unknown product company can beat an ₹18 LPA GCC offer for your *next* jump's base.

**Pipeline compression + follow-up (30 min):**
- [ ] Review every row. Any company silent 5+ business days after a round → polite follow-up today: "Hi [Name], following up on my [role] interview on [date]. I remain very interested and happy to provide any additional information. Looking forward to next steps."
- [ ] Already have an offer/near-offer? Contact every preferred-but-slower company proactively: *"I've received an offer from another company with a deadline of [date]. I'm genuinely more interested in [preferred company] — is there any way to accelerate the timeline?"*

**Pipeline:** This is the compression day. Keep "available to join immediately" framing in every recruiter touch. Log all nudges and responses within 24h.

**EOD check:**
- [ ] Can you bridge your experience to every tech gap in your active JDs?
- [ ] Does every active company have tailored questions you'll ask?
- [ ] Can you deliver the first-offer "buy time" line without hesitation?
- [ ] DSA tree solve done?
