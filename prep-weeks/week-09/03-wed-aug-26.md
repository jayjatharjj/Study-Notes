# Week 9 · Day 3 — Wed Aug 26 — Behavioural + HM Round Prep

📌 **Today:** Lock the behavioural arsenal. Six STAR skeletons, a metric in every one, each deliverable in 90–120 seconds. Then prep the hiring-manager round: 3-year vision, first-30-days, and a tailored "why this company" for each active loop.

**DSA maintenance (~30 min):**
- [ ] Two **LC Easy** problems from categories you haven't touched this week — warm-up, not study. 10 min total max.

**Primary activity:**

**Behavioural drill (45 min) — 6 STAR skeletons (S/T/A/R, one page each):**
- [ ] 1. **Performance improvement** — Smart360 60s → 2–3s. Lead: S3/Redis caching + N+1 fix.
- [ ] 2. **Technical decision with trade-offs** — event-driven / strangler-fig migration. Lead: what you chose NOT to do and why.
- [ ] 3. **Something broke in production** — OOMKilled pod, DB migration blocking readiness probe, pipeline rate-limit failure. No drama? Be honest about scale, precise about debugging.
- [ ] 4. **Influence without authority** — convincing a team to change technical direction.
- [ ] 5. **Learning on the fly** — a tech you picked up fast and what you shipped with it.
- [ ] 6. **Proudest project** — Deep Fathom: full-stack ownership, IaC, LLM async, FedRAMP alignment.
- [ ] Say each aloud, timed. Target **90–120s**. Under 90s = skipping impact; over 2 min = narrating process, not outcome.
- [ ] **Number check:** every story has ≥ 1 metric. "We improved performance" is not STAR. "Latency dropped 96%, 60s → 2–3s" is.

**HM round prep (30 min) — crisp answers for:**
- [ ] "Where in 3 years?" — technical leadership + product impact, not just a title.
- [ ] "What do you look for in a team?" — specific: "proper code review, takes architecture seriously" > "collaborative and supportive."
- [ ] "A topic you've been exploring on your own?" — one real answer (K8s Gateway API vs Ingress, LLM function-calling patterns, Bicep AVM modules).
- [ ] "First 30 days?" — "understand the architecture, shadow deploys, read incident retros, find the one thing I can contribute by week 2 without breaking trust."
- [ ] "Why this company over others?" — MUST be tailored. Generic answers kill offers.

**Pipeline:** Confirm every round on the calendar has prep time blocked the evening before. Log all interactions within 24h.

**EOD check:**
- [ ] Every STAR story has a number — yes/no?
- [ ] Can you answer "Why us specifically?" for each active company without hesitation — yes/no?
- [ ] DSA warm-ups done?
