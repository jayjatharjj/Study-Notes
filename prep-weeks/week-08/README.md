## 🧠 This Week's Operating Principles

- **Scheduled loops override new volume.** A live loop you nail beats ten cold applies you rushed. Applications drop to an 8–12 trickle with heavy follow-up; prep for the next round always wins the time conflict.
- **Rehearse the right deep-dive *before* each round.** Perf-heavy role → Smart360; infra/DevOps → Deep Fathom; async/LLM → WebX. Say the talk-track aloud the morning of, so it's fresh, not recalled.
- **Decompress and reset between rounds.** A 15-minute reset (away from the screen) is the difference between a sharp third loop and a fried one. Treat recovery as part of the performance.
- **Numbers in every behavioral answer.** "60s → 2–3s, a 96% reduction, 80% fewer S3 calls" proves you owned it. Every STAR story lands a specific number.
- **Immediate availability shortens the close.** When timelines come up, "available to join immediately" is a concrete advantage in a clustered, competitive window.
