# Week 10 — Offers + Negotiation (Aug 31–Sep 6, 2026)

> You've stopped performing and started converting. Every conversation is now a business negotiation — and you have more leverage than you think, especially because you can **join immediately**. The goal is not *an* offer; it's the *best* offer, evaluated on the right numbers, with every lever pulled.

> 📨 **Apps & referrals this week:** No new applications — convert the pipeline; compress decisions into a tight window for leverage. See the [cadence & tracker](../applications-and-referrals.md).

## 🎯 Week Goal

1. Know your floor / target / stretch cold; never negotiate from memory.
2. Deflect the CTC question; counter cleanly (specific number, one reason); ask for joining bonus / ESOP where the band is capped.
3. Evaluate on **in-hand + guaranteed cash**, not headline CTC. Use **immediate availability** as a closing lever.
