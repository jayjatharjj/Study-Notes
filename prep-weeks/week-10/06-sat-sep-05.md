# Week 10 · Day 6 — Sat Sep 5 — Deliver Counters + Per-Company Close Prep

📌 **Today:** Deliver the counters you drafted Thursday, and prep the final close conversations. Calls/emails go out today; movement gets logged today.

**DSA maintenance (~15 min):** Light — keep the streak.

**Primary activity:**

**Deliver the counters (60 min):**
- [ ] For each below-target offer, deliver the written counter — specific number, one reason, no ultimatum (script in [Day 4](04-thu-sep-03.md)). Prefer a call; follow with a one-line email summary.
- [ ] Where the base band is capped, pivot to the joining-bonus / ESOP ask, framed on **immediate availability** ("no notice-period lag on my side").
- [ ] After you counter, **stop talking** — let silence do the work. Don't negotiate against yourself.

**Compress the timeline (45 min):**
- [ ] Tell every live company your decision window (e.g., "deciding by mid next week"). Use any offer in hand to accelerate preferred-but-slower companies.
- [ ] For the offers you'd accept, confirm exactly what's outstanding (written letter, BGV list, start date) so nothing stalls next week.

**Per-company close prep (30 min):**
- [ ] For your top 1–2 offers, list the open questions you need answered before saying yes (team, on-call, growth path, WFH policy, ESOP details).

**Pipeline:** Log every counter delivered + response. Mark which offers are "accept-ready."

**EOD check:**
- [ ] All counters delivered? · [ ] Decision window communicated to every live company? · [ ] Top offers' open questions listed?
