# Week 10 · Day 5 — Fri Sep 4 — Evaluate Offers on In-Hand + Guaranteed Cash

📌 **Today:** Rank every live offer on the numbers that actually matter — **monthly in-hand** and **guaranteed annual cash** — not the headline CTC. Two ₹22 LPA offers can differ by lakhs in what lands in your account.

**DSA maintenance (~15 min):** Light — one warm-up.

**Primary activity:**

**Build the comparison sheet (60 min):** for each offer compute:
- [ ] **Monthly in-hand** — base after the fixed/variable split and statutory deductions (PF, professional tax, TDS). Variable that "could be" 20% is *not* in-hand.
- [ ] **Guaranteed annual cash** = base + any fixed/contractual component, excluding variable and ESOP (paper money for years).
- [ ] **Total CTC** — for reference only, ranked last.
- [ ] Note: designation/band (affects every future raise), ESOP vesting + realistic value, joining bonus + clawback, location/hybrid.

**Apply the lens (30 min):**
- [ ] Rank offers by guaranteed cash + in-hand first, then growth/scope. A higher-base unknown product co often beats a variable-heavy big name — especially with no incoming salary to bridge.
- [ ] Flag any offer below your **floor number** — that's a decline regardless of brand.
- [ ] For each below-target offer, confirm the counter is drafted (from Thu) and ready to deliver Saturday.

**Pipeline:** Update the tracker with the computed in-hand/guaranteed-cash for each offer. Keep "available immediately" in every recruiter touch.

**EOD check:**
- [ ] Every offer has a computed in-hand + guaranteed-cash number? · [ ] Offers ranked on those, not CTC? · [ ] Any below-floor offer flagged?
