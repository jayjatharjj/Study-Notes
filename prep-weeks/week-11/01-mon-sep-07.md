# Week 11 · Day 1 — Mon Sep 07 — Counter-Offer Execution + The Silence Rule

📌 **Today:** Stop studying, start closing. Finalise the counter script for every live offer below target (specific number, one reason, then silence), then actually deliver it. The lever this week is that you've already resigned — **immediate availability** is your closing plus, not a wind-down. Use it to close, not to give away.

**DSA maintenance (light/optional, ~15 min):**
- [ ] Optional — only if a backup loop is still live. One easy LC to stay warm. Otherwise skip; you're past the study phase.

**Primary activity:**

**Pre-day check (5 min):**
- [ ] Open the tracker. List every live offer and its gap to target. Mark which need a counter today.

**Morning: Finalise the Counter (30 min):**
- [ ] For each live offer below target, finalise the counter script from Week 10. Structure: (1) genuine enthusiasm; (2) one real reason you want to join; (3) the gap, anchored to market or a competing offer; (4) a specific number (not a range); (5) confidence it can be resolved.
  - Template: *"I'm genuinely excited about [Company] — [product/team/challenge] is exactly the space I want to grow into. I've reviewed the offer and want to be straightforward: the base is slightly below what I need. Based on market data and a competing offer I'm evaluating, I was expecting around ₹[target]. Could you revisit the base to ₹[target]? I'm confident we can close this quickly — [Company] is my first choice."*
- [ ] Principles: a range "₹20–22" is always heard as ₹20, so name one number. Give only ONE reason. No deadline ultimatum on the first counter. Frame it as a solvable problem, not a confrontation.

**Midday: Deliver + The Silence Rule (45 min):**
- [ ] Make the counter call today (or send it, if the recruiter prefers email). After you name the number — **stop talking.** The first person to speak after a proposal loses ground. Count to 5 mentally if needed. Their silence is calculation, not rejection.
- [ ] **Never negotiate against yourself.** If they say "let me check," do NOT add "but I could do less." Wait for their response.
- [ ] **BATNA check before every call:** know your best alternative (current job, or your strongest competing offer). The stronger your alternative, the harder you can push. With a weak alternative, push respectfully but don't bluff.

---

**Pipeline:** Counters come first today. Respond to recruiters within hours. Log every counter sent — number named, reason given, their reaction — in the tracker the same day.

**EOD check:**
- [ ] Did you name a specific number and exactly one reason?
- [ ] Did you hold silence after the ask without filling it?
- [ ] Did you avoid negotiating against yourself?
- [ ] Every counter logged in the tracker?
