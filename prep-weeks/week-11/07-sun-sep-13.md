# Week 11 · Day 7 — Sun Sep 13 — Decision Lock + Week 12 Plan

📌 **Today:** Lock the decision (or the dated path to it), decline the rest gracefully, and set up the final close/buffer week.

**Primary activity:**

**Lock it in (30 min):**
- [ ] If you have the countersigned letter: you're done — this is the win. Note the start date and breathe.
- [ ] If you're one step away: write the exact remaining steps + dates for the lead offer, and the single thing that would change your mind.

**Decline gracefully (30 min):**
- [ ] For offers you're turning down, send a short, warm, specific note — you may want these companies for your *next* move. Keep the door open.
- [ ] Thank every referrer and helper by name. This network is your head start next time.

**Set up Week 12 (close / buffer) (20 min):**
- [ ] Pre-read [Week 12](../week-12/). If accepted → it's logistics + a pre-joining ramp. If not yet → it's the fallback buffer: re-engage the [Floor ladder](../applications-and-referrals.md), nudge every late-stage loop.
- [ ] Reminder: offers realistically cluster late-Sep → late-Oct. A slip into early October is normal, not failure.

**EOD check:**
- [ ] Decision locked or dated? · [ ] Other offers declined warmly? · [ ] Referrers thanked?
