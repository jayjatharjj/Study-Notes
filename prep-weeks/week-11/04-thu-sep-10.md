# Week 11 · Day 4 — Thu Sep 10 — Final Comparison + The Decision

📌 **Today:** Build the weighted comparison with post-negotiation numbers, rank on guaranteed cash + in-hand (not headline CTC), and pick the winner. Then write your one-paragraph "why I chose this" note. Treat **immediate availability** as a real plus in the start-date-fit row — it's a closing lever, not a constraint.
