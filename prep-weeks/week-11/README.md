# Week 11 — Convert + Accept (Sep 7–13, 2026)

> This is the week you stop negotiating and start deciding. You have offers — or you're days from them. Negotiate hard, get the offers to overlap into one decision window, hold the line against pressure tactics, and accept the best one cleanly. Because you can **join immediately**, you carry a lever most candidates don't — use it to close, not to give away.
